# TASKS.md

A simple place to collect product ideas, coding tasks, business tasks, and experiments for **Today's Specials**.

## Current Ideas And Tasks

1. Restaurant email subscriber automation
   - Explore how restaurants can email specials to the platform.
   - Convert incoming emails into draft specials.
   - Add a simple approval flow before publishing.
   - Consider sending subscribers a daily or weekly digest of relevant specials.

2. Email marketing automation for special gatherings
   - Plan email flows for events, private dinners, tasting nights, holiday meals, and local gatherings.
   - Let restaurants promote special gatherings to followers.
   - Keep emails simple, local, and useful.
   - Avoid making this a complex marketing dashboard too early.

3. Explore pricing models
   - Test simple restaurant subscriptions.
   - Consider pricing tiers around claimed pages, direct posting, featured specials, and follower alerts.
   - Start with the README range of $29-$99/month.
   - Compare subscription, featured placement, local sponsorship, and per-market licensing options.
